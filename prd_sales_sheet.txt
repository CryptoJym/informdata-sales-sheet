# InformData Sales Collateral PRD

## Objective
Produce a sales-ready pricing sheet for Vuplicity.com covering InformData services, in-house pricing (InformData cost + $1 AI/SaaS margin), competitive MSRPs, and key use cases.

## Key Requirements
- Gather approved InformData service costs from finance sources.
- Add $1 per line item to represent AI+SaaS overhead.
- Document competitor MSRPs for analogous services.
- Summarize use cases and relevance for buyers.
- Prepare content for export to Gamma presentation for the sales team.
